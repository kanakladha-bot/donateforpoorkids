Who We Are

Giving Every Child a Fair Chance

DonateForPoorKids is a grassroots NGO based in Jaipur, working to uplift the lives of underprivileged children through sustainable, community-driven programmes.

Award WinningBest NGO Rajasthan 2024

Our Mission

To ensure every underprivileged child in Rajasthan has access to nutritious food, quality education, adequate clothing, and compassionate healthcare — no exceptions.

Our Vision

A future where no child goes to bed hungry or grows up without education — where poverty is never a barrier to potential.

Why We Exist

Millions of Indian children still lack basics. We fill that gap — one family, one meal, one school kit at a time — with full transparency and accountability.
